Our Focus

Following our work over the years, we have identified four key areas that would enable people and businesses thrive in Africa. Namely: Identity, Learning, Payment and Data.

Identity

Enroll & Verify Anyone

We believe that no society can truly grow her community without first identifying the people that belong to the community. Identity is the bedrock to planning, financial inclusion and targeting.

Learning

Quality Learning For All

A society can not develop beyond the capacity of its people. Technology can enable the delivery of quality education to various segments of society at scale.

Payment

A Bank On Every Phone

We are consistently building and designing solutions that help individuals and organisations use our payment platform to create custom experiences for their businesses and consumers in the most inclusiveand effective ways.

Data

Insights For Decision Making

Data can be used by businesses to make significant and more educated choices; which to a great extent, means improved business operations, expansion and development of new models and decrease in costs among others.
